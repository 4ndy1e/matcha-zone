Matcha

Taking the time to educate and familiarize yourself with the different grades of matcha and their unique properties will help you find the best match, depending on how you plan to enjoy and use it.

The Different Grades of Matcha

Ceremonial

Hand-picked and made from the youngest first-harvest green tea leaves, highest quality matcha available.

Daily

Often from a combination of the first and second harvest or just the second harvest of young green tea leaves.

Culinary

Commonly comes from older green tea leaves than those chosen for ceremonial or daily matcha – often from the second, third or even fourth harvest.
